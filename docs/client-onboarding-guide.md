# Client Onboarding Guide
**OTB Pipeline — Revoice Studio**

---

## How to Get Started

You do not need an account created for you in advance. You can register your company yourself in under 2 minutes.

---

## Step 1 — Register Your Company

Go to:

```
https://www.boothop.com/onboard
```

Fill in:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Company Name | Your business name |
| Contact Name | Your name |
| Email | Your business email |
| Password | Choose a password (remember this — it's how you log in) |
| WhatsApp | Your WhatsApp number (optional, for notifications) |
| Plan | Leave as **Basic** unless told otherwise |

Click **Create Account**. You will see a confirmation screen with your login link.

---

## Step 2 — Complete the Setup Wizard

After registering, go to:

```
https://www.boothop.com/client-onboarding
```

This is a 5-step wizard that configures your account:

1. **Company details** — confirm your name and contact
2. **Branding** — upload your logo and brand colours
3. **Content preferences** — the type of videos you want produced
4. **Delivery** — where finished videos should be sent (email / WhatsApp / TikTok)
5. **Confirm** — review and activate

You only need to do this once.

---

## Step 3 — Log In to Your Dashboard

Once setup is complete, log in at:

```
https://www.boothop.com/login
```

Use the **email** and **password** you chose in Step 1.

---

## What You Can Do in the Dashboard

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Revoice Studio** | Upload your own voice recording to replace the AI voice on any video |
| **Bake & Post** | Merge your voice with the video and post it directly to TikTok or Instagram |
| **Bake History** | See all your past bakes with status and download links |
| **Music** | Choose background music from the library or search YouTube |

---

## Revoice Studio — How to Use It

1. Log in to your dashboard
2. Choose a video slot (S2, S3, or S4)
3. Click **Re-voice** — a script will appear for you to read
4. Record yourself reading the script using the Telegram bot (if connected) or upload a voice note
5. Pick background music from the list
6. Click **Bake** — the system mixes your voice with the music and video
7. Review the result, then click **Post to TikTok** or **Post to Instagram**
