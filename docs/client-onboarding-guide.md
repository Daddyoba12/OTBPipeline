# Client Onboarding Guide
**BootHop Pipeline — Commander & Revoice Studio**
*Updated June 2026*

---

## Overview

As a BootHop Pipeline client you get two connected tools:

| Tool | What it's for | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **Commander** | Manage your music, view your account | `www.boothop.com/commander` |
| **Revoice Studio** | Upload your voice, bake and post videos | `www.boothop.com/login` |

Getting started takes four steps: fill in your onboarding form → register your accounts → add your music → start revoicing.

---

## Step 1 — Complete Your Onboarding Form

Go to:
```
https://www.boothop.com/client-onboarding
```

This is a 5-step wizard that configures your pipeline. You only do this once.

| Step | What you're filling in |
|---|---|
| 1. Business Identity | Your company name, what you do, location, target audience |
| 2. Content Strategy | Marketing focus, content tone, brand voice notes |
| 3. Platforms & Credentials | Which platforms (TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube) + login credentials for each |
| 4. Notifications | Telegram bot or WhatsApp for pipeline alerts |
| 5. Generate | Preview your config, download it, and register your pipeline |

At Step 5, click **Save & Register Client** — your setup is saved to our server automatically. Also click **Download config.env** and keep that file safe.

> **No password needed here** — this form sets up your pipeline content config, not your login account. You'll set passwords in the next steps.

---

## Step 2 — Register Your Two Accounts

You need two separate accounts: one for **Revoice Studio** and one for **Commander**. Do both now.

### Revoice Studio account
```
https://www.boothop.com/onboard
```
Fill in your company name, contact name, email, and choose a **password**. This is the email + password you'll use to log into Revoice Studio.

### Commander account
```
https://www.boothop.com/commander → Create Account
```

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Company / Business Name | Your business name exactly as you trade |
| Company ID | A short ID you'll use to log in (e.g. `acme-media`) — lowercase, hyphens only, cannot be changed |
| Contact Name | Your name |
| Email | Your business email — needed for password resets |
| Password | Choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters) |
| Plan | Leave as **Basic** unless your account manager told you otherwise |

Click **Create Account**. You will be taken straight to your Commander dashboard.

> **Note:** Your Company ID is your permanent login username for Commander. Choose something short and memorable — it cannot be changed later.
> You now have two separate passwords: one for Revoice Studio, one for Commander. Keep both safe.

---

## Step 3 — Set Up Your Music

In Commander, go to the **Music** tab.

You have three ways to add music to your videos:

### Option A — BootHop Library
Browse our curated library of royalty-free tracks. Filter by genre (Afrobeats, Lo-fi, Electronic, etc.). Click **+ Add** on any track.

### Option B — Search YouTube
Type an artist or track name (e.g. *Burna Boy afrobeats 2025*) and click **Search**. Click **+ Add** on any result.

### Option C — Paste a YouTube Link
Paste any YouTube URL or video ID directly into the link field. Click **Look up**, then **+ Add**.

Your tracks appear in the **My Tracks** tab. The pipeline picks them up automatically — no further action needed.

## Step 4 — Revoice &amp; Post Videos

Once your pipeline is live and videos are being produced:
```
https://www.boothop.com/login
```

Use your **email** and the **Revoice Studio password** you set at `www.boothop.com/onboard`. This is separate from your Commander password.

### What you can do in Revoice Studio:

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Re-voice** | Upload your own voice recording to replace the AI voice on any video slot |
| **Bake & Post** | The system mixes your voice + your chosen music + the video and posts it |
| **Bake History** | See all past bakes with status and download links |

### How to Re-voice a video:
1. Log in → choose a video slot (S2, S3, or S4)
2. Click **Re-voice** — you'll see the script to read
3. Record yourself reading the script and upload it as a voice note
4. Pick background music from the list
5. Click **Bake** — the system processes it (usually under 2 minutes)
6. Review, then click **Post to TikTok** or **Post to Instagram**

## Signing In to Commander Later

Go to `www.boothop.com/commander` and click **Sign In**.

Enter your **Company ID** and **password**.

If you forget your password, click **Reset Password**, enter your email, and follow the link sent to your inbox.
